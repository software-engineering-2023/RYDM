We support all types of transfers

Internal Transfers

Movement of funds between accounts held within the same financial institution or bank.

Domestic Transfers

Transfer of funds between accounts held within the same country. It refers to transactions conducted within the domestic banking system, where the sender and recipient of the funds are located in the same country.

International Transfers

Movement of funds between accounts held in different countries. It refers to transactions conducted across national borders, where the sender and recipient of the funds are located in different countries.
